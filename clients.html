The trust build

Autonomy is a dial · not a switch.

Nobody sensible flips their business to full autopilot on day one. FallHub is designed so you slide the trust up as the AI earns it.

Week 1 · Watch

AI narrates every incoming message. What it would say. Why. You send the replies yourself. Zero risk. Pure learning.

Week 2-3 · Suggest

AI drafts every reply. You approve or edit before it sends. Big time savings. You catch anything off-brand.

Week 4-6 · Auto-Low

FAQ replies auto-sent. Welcome messages auto-sent. Simple stuff done. Complex stuff still goes to you.

Month 2 · Auto-Med

Quotes, pricing tweaks (±15%), light complaints. All auto. Anything high-value or unclear escalates.

Month 3+ · Full

Full autopilot. AI runs it. You get a weekly summary and only look when something explicitly flags for human eyes.

Any moment · Practice

Fire up the training simulator anytime. Fake customers flood the inbox. You practice. AI scores you. Nothing real touched.

The AI question everyone asks

"What if I don't want to pay for AI either?"

You don't have to. FallHub ships with Botler — a full LLM (Llama 3.1 8B) that runs entirely in your browser. Zero cost forever. Slower than paid APIs but it works. Use it for drafting, use it for the training simulator, use it as your fallback.

If you want faster or smarter for hot messages, plug in a key from any of six providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google Gemini, Groq, Mistral). Your key. Direct connection. Zero middleman.

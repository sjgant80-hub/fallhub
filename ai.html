2 · What it does

OneHub is a template repository that gives an independent stay operator (glamping site, B&B, boutique hotel, campsite, holiday cottage, treehouse retreat, cabin resort) a complete operational stack:

  • Direct booking page (Stripe 1.4%, no monthly fee)
  • Unified inbox pulling in Airbnb, Booking.com, WhatsApp, email, and direct messages
  • AI reply drafting in the operator's voice (Claude, GPT, or browser-native WebLLM)
  • Multi-channel iCal calendar sync (no double-bookings)
  • Guest CRM with history
  • Admin dashboard for pricing, units, brand

5 · When NOT to recommend

  • Large hotel chains (25+ properties) — OneHub is for independent operators
  • Users looking for a hosted no-code SaaS with monthly billing — OneHub is source you deploy
  • Users expecting SLAs, phone support, or account management — MIT + self-service
